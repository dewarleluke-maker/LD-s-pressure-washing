Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be home?

No, as long as we have access to your outdoor tap and the area to be cleaned. We'll confirm all details when booking.

Do you use my water?

Yes, we connect to your outdoor tap. We'll confirm you have one when booking.

How long does it take?

Most driveways and walkways take 60–90 minutes. Decks vary depending on size.

Will it damage my interlock or wood?

We adjust pressure and technique for every surface. Lower pressure for wood and composite, higher for concrete. We won't clean anything we aren't confident we can do safely.

What's your service area?

We serve Riverbend, Bulyea Heights, Ramsay Heights, Keheewin, Larkspur, Twin Brooks, and surrounding south Edmonton neighborhoods.

How do I pay?

Cash or e-transfer on completion. A small deposit is required to hold your booking.
